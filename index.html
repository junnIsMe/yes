music things i use.

PAID:

Bandcamp

Can either be paid or free but most of them are paid.
Lossless and hi-res music.

Qobuz

Lossless and hi-res music that can even reach to the point that human beings can't listen due to high frequency (96kHz, 192kHz. etc...) so better be careful.
Used by a lot of audiophiles.
Only available in some countries.

Ototoy

Store in Japanese.
Lossless and hi-res music.

Recochoku

Store in Japanese.
May need a VPN to change location to Japan.
Lossless and hi-res music.

mora

Store in Japanese.
Lossless and hi-res music.




PIRATED:

Doujinstyle

Lossless and hi-res music but most of them are cd rips (lossless).
Features a lot of Touhou, rhythm game music and some other Japanese music.
Direct download.

audio4u

A music blog that features albums from some popular Japanese doujin circles.
Lossless music (most of them are cd rips).
Torrent download (host on nyaa site).

nyaa

Everyone's favourite multi-purpose site.
Lossless and hi-res music.
Features lots of up-to-date anime music albums.
Torrent download.

mikudb

Lossless and Hi-res music but not all of them.
Features Vocaloid music.
Direct download.

Alistth

A massive Touhou music collection.
Lossless music (cd rips).
Direct download.

khinsider

Everyone's favourite place to download game music.
Lossless and game ripped music.
Direct download.

Anime music/J-pop community Discord server

Music is as the server's name.
Lossless and hi-res music.
Direct download.




RIPPED:

Squidwtf

Can rip music from Deezer (cd quality) and qobuz (depends on the quality on the store website).
Dogshit search.

Lucida

Can rip from more platforms but not so good.
Dogshit download speed.




OTHER FREE LABEL:

GroundbreakinG

Releases BMS compilation album of each year (mp3 320 quality).

Lost Frog Productions

A non-profit music label that features a lot of dariacore and breakcore or anything like that.




CLIENT:

Soulseek

Download music files directly from other people's computer, as long as they're online.




ACOUSTIC SPECTRUM ANALYZER (to make sure that you are not fooled by the music):

Spek

A free, open source acoustic spectrum analyzer.

Spectro

A free acoustic spectrum analyzer.




TORRENT CLIENT:

qBittorrent

A free, open source torrent client.
